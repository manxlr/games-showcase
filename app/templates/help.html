Games Showcase

Welcome

Your personal, self-hosted game journal: track what you play, rate it, collect art, and export a beautiful PDF of your library.

Getting started

  1. Create an account — Register with a username, email, and password.
  2. Log in — You’ll land on your library dashboard.
  3. Add a game — Use + Add Game, pick status, categories, and optional details.
  4. Images — Drag & drop up to 20 images per game (cover + screenshots). First image can become the cover.
  5. Filters — Use status chips, category chips, search, and favorites to narrow the list.
  6. Export PDF — Exports only the games currently visible after your filters (high-quality layout).

Tips

  • Click the star on a card to toggle favorite without opening the editor.
  • Use Light / Dark mode (top-right) — your choice is remembered in this browser.
  • Categories are fixed tags — pick as many as apply (including Other).
